Welcome to MoviesXclusively4U! ... a movie recommendation system.

Our number one goal is to find you movies that you will love and enjoy, without the extra leg work of having to search through millions of options for the perfect watch.

We have built our recommendations in two ways - Movies specific to your age and gender or all movies regardless of age and gender. Furthermore, a recommendation can be given collaboratively based on user similarity or item similarity.

  • User-based recommendations are calculated based on which movies your top 10 most similar users have liked.
  • Item-based recommendations are calculated based other movies similar to the ones you have alread viewed.

We hope that you find our site useful and will enjoy watching the movies recommended for you!
